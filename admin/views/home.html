  • Fastest
    Delivery MCB is the fastest way to deliver the parcels. We deliver parcels one to one via our verified Transporters. MCB delivers all parcels by Air only.

  • Cost
    Saving MCB is cheaper as compared to other Air cargo services in Market for same day delivery of parcels. All the parcels shall be delivered within 24 hours in the Country.

  • Extra
    Income MCB is a social courier service, where the sender pays and Transporter receives. Money paid by sender is shared directly to Transporter. Become a transporter to earn additional income, while traveling.

  • Efficient Transport
    Channel MCB is the most efficient transport channel. Sender always know When, How and Who will carry your parcel. You are always in touch with your parcel directly or indirectly.

  • make New
    Friends MCB helps to bring sender, transporter and receiver together, which brings people closer. You get opportunity to meet new people and make new friends, who may belong to your home town or travelling to your city frequently.

  • Collaboration
    Mutual Benefit MCB is a collaborative model, where the money is given to the transporter who provides the services. MCB brings sender and transporter together to help each other.
