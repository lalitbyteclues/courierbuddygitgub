Senders Good Practice and Recommendation
Make sure you pack the goods in such a way that courier buddy can easily inspect and aware about the content in package before he accepts the package. This keep the mutual interest of both sender and courier buddy intact.

Packing is very important for shipping your package safely without damage. Avoid sending the goods which are fragile and easily damageable without the consent and acceptance of your courier buddy.

You shall be informed, once your delivery order is accepted and matched with your courier buddy. Coordinate with your courier buddy to handover the package to courier buddy. Prefer to handover the package near to the departure date and time of the courier buddy.

Send the photo of package and your courier buddy to receiver and inform the receiver about the packing details.

Make sure the goods you are sending are not prohibited by law or other regulations to be owned or distributed within country and across the borders like Drugs, Illegal items, Weapons, Chemicals etc.

Different country have different customs guidelines and guidelines for prohibited goods. Make sure you read the customs guidelines for the destination country carefully and follow the guidelines.

Goods which are liable for customs in destination country shall be clearly mentioned and bring in knowledge of your courier buddy. Make sure you have agreement with your courier buddy to pay the customs duty and get the goods clear. MCB is not directly responsible for any dispute involving customs or customs duty payment.

Avoid sending fragile, difficult to handle, expensive and valuable items with MCB.
