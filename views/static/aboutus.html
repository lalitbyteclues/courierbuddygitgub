About us

Mycourierbuddy is a person to person social courier platform which was founded in July 2016. By seamlessly connecting travelers (Transporter) with the Senders, we connect people to ship parcels faster and in person. We bring people in a circle to help each other in order to send the parcels faster, save some time & money, make new friends and over and above all help each other on our planet. We make the shipping of parcels possible around the world at the fastest pace. Whether you want to send some sweets or document to your children studying overseas or you want to send wedding dress for your best friend at last minute. MCB provides platform to help each other and mutually benefit each other.
