Transporters Good Practice and Recommendation
Once you confirm your travel plan, book the tickets and receive the PNR Number and Ticket Number from Airlines, then create your trips in My-Trips in MCB portal or App. Plan your luggage for your personal use for trip and estimate how much you can carry additional as MCB transporter.

Familiarize yourself with local customs regulations common at destination. Make sure package always comply as per the local customs regulations to be carried to transit and destination country.

Once your trip is saved in MCB, search for suitable deliveries for your destination, and confirm deliveries which are suitable for transportation as per your capacity and convenience.

Keep in touch with sender by mail, SMS or phone call, discuss exact details like weight and dimensions about the package. Make an appointment with sender and finalize about venue, date & time to receive the package.

Coordinate with sender to receive the package as per both of your convenience. While receiving the delivery, make sure you take photo of package along with the sender for future reference.

MCB Transporter shall make sure the content of the package is as per the discussion and specifications about the package category, weight and dimension roughly matches with the online order. Upon picking update the status as "Picked" in MCB app.

Keep the package safely in hand bag or check-in baggage as per size or convenience to carry. Prefer always to scan the package at airport before you check in or carry in hand bag.

Remember to coordinate with receiver buddy before you fly so that it's easy to identify and hand order the packet at destination. If possible ask for contact details and other identifications of the receiver.

On meeting receiver hand over the package safely and remember to update the delivered status on MCB app either with your mobile or with receivers mobile. At the same time remember to take a photo of delivered package with your receiver buddy for records in your mobile.

Request your sender and receiver buddy to update the feedback for this delivery.
